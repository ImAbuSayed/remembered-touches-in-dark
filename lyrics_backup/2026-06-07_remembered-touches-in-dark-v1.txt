[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows fall across the wooden floor
Your fingers trace a memory on my door
I can still feel the fire from the spark
These remembered touches in the dark

[Verse 1]
A quiet moon is shining through the pine
Pouring out a glass of sweet red wine
Your breath is catching right against my skin
This is where the midnight starts to spin

[Pre-Chorus]
Pulling you closer, taking it slow
Got nowhere else we need to go
The embers fade but we are burning bright
Getting lost inside the quiet night

[Chorus]
Oh, I want to wrap you in my arms
Keep you safe from all the outside harms
Let my lips wander, leave a gentle mark
With these remembered touches in the dark

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, reaching out and pulling you near
Whispering the secrets you want to hear

[Verse 2]
The rustle of the sheets is our only sound
With every heartbeat, solid ground
Your eyes are pulling me into the deep
A silent promise that I want to keep

[Bridge]

Loving you long after the stars are gone
